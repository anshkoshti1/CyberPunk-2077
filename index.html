About Cyberpunk 2077

Welcome to Cyberpunk 2077, an open-world action-adventure RPG set in the dystopian metropolis of Night City. Dive into a world where humanity and technology collide, and only the strongest survive.

Cyberpunk 2077 is a story-driven, open-world game developed by CD PROJEKT RED. You play as V, a mercenary navigating a high-stakes world of crime and corporate espionage. Customize your character, choose your path, and shape your destiny in a city that knows no mercy.

Key Features:

  • Immersive Storyline: Experience a gripping narrative that adapts to your choices.
  • Open World Exploration: Roam freely in the vast and detailed world of Night City.
  • Character Customization: Tailor your character's appearance, skills, and cyberware.
  • Dynamic Combat: Engage in intense gunfights, melee combat, and hacking.
  • Rich Visuals: Enjoy stunning graphics and a highly detailed environment.
